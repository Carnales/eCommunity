About Us.

When we think of winter, we think about two things: the holidays, and community. While many rejoice during winter with Christmas and gifts, many others find it to be trying times for them and their business. As a result, we decided to unite the local community under one spectacular online store where local businesses can spread holiday love with products for sale, and families can help local bussinesses out by buying from the local stores for holiday shopping, aided through this online platform, the winter eCommunity.
